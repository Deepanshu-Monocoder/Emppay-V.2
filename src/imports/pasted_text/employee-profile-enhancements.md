EXPORT SYSTEM & EMPLOYEE PROFILE ENHANCEMENT REQUIREMENTS

IMPORTANT:
The Excel and PDF exports must closely match the previously approved design previews.

The goal is to make exports feel like a premium HR/Payroll SaaS product, not a simple table dump.

==================================================
EXCEL EXPORT REDESIGN
=====================

Create a professional multi-sheet Excel workbook.

Workbook Name Example:

Arjun_Sharma_Report.xlsx

Export Structure:

Sheet 1 – Dashboard / Employee Summary
Sheet 2 – Attendance Records
Sheet 3 – Leave History
Sheet 4 – Payroll History
Sheet 5 – Attendance Calendar

---

## SHEET 1 – DASHBOARD

This should look like a management dashboard.

Include:

Company Logo
Company Name
Report Period
Generated Date & Time

Employee Profile Card:

* Profile Photo
* Name
* Employee ID
* Department
* Salary
* Joining Date

Summary Cards:

* Present Days
* Half Days
* Paid Leaves
* Sick Leaves
* Unpaid Leaves
* Other Absences
* Attendance Percentage

Attendance Summary Table

Payroll Summary Table

Attendance Breakdown Chart

Leave Breakdown Chart

Salary Trend Chart

Use professional formatting:

* Frozen headers
* Auto-sized columns
* Color-coded status indicators
* Print-ready layout

---

## SHEET 2 – ATTENDANCE RECORDS

Include only meaningful attendance entries.

Do NOT fill the sheet with endless "Not Marked" rows.

Columns:

Date
Day
Status
Remarks
Reason

Status colors:

Green = Present
Yellow = Half Day
Blue = Paid Leave
Purple = Sick Leave
Red = Unpaid Leave
Orange = Other
Gray = Holiday

---

## SHEET 3 – LEAVE HISTORY

Columns:

Date
Leave Type
Duration
Reason
Remarks

Only leave-related records.

---

## SHEET 4 – PAYROLL HISTORY

Columns:

Month
Payable Days
Gross Salary
Deductions
Net Salary

Add salary trend chart.

---

## SHEET 5 – ATTENDANCE CALENDAR

Display a monthly attendance calendar.

Dates should be color-coded based on attendance status.

Include legend.

This sheet should visually match the employee profile calendar.
